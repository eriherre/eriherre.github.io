About Me


Hello, my name is Gonzalo, and I am passionate about designing and developing innovative human-computer interactions using cutting-edge technologies. I genuinely believe we are transforming the world by creating more sophisticated and immersive ways to better understand our reality, and being part of that is fantastic.

I have a background in Psychology, but I’m also passionate about exploring how technology can enhance people’s lives. To the cause, during the past years, I had the great opportunity to expand the boundaries of my knowledge while completing my master’s degree in Human Interface Technology. Since then, I have studied and learned to combine and support principles from both disciplines. So it is not unlikely for me to feel that I’m usually placed in the middle of two different worlds. Nevertheless, that’s what I enjoy the most. I like to think of myself as a bridge or point of connection between people and technology.

I have years of experience developing interactive computer applications based on creativity and a human-centred approach. My specialities include learning design principles, problem-solving, teamwork, programming languages, human-computer interfaces, games, e-learning modules and immersive experiences.
