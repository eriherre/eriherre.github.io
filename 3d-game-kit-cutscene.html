3D Game Kit Cutscene

This prototype was an assignment developed as part of my Unity 3D Artist Course. Two primary sequences comprise the cutscene. The first part shows a military base located in the middle of nowhere, surrounded by the alien vegetation of an unknown planet. A fleet of spaceships is visible from the top. A close-up view shows a commander and her trusty adventure companion preparing for a new mission. This section ends with the ship’s takeoff heading towards planet X. The second part shows the ship flying into an isolated area of planet X. This area is home to a mysterious temple in the middle of a giant lake that stands in ruin. A colossal doorway is rippling with an odd blue light at the far end of the ruins. The ship coasts along the water and travels up the destroyed temple causeway before landing on the most solid ground nearest to the doorway. Here the cutscene ends.


This cutscene was developed using the Unity3D engine. All the assets are part of the 3D Game Kit developed by Unity Technologies. My responsibilities were to determine methods for creating simple 3D and 2D keyframed animations and expand upon creative techniques to sequence animations and camera movements using Unity’s timeline.
