Listeria Awareness - e-learning course

This e-learning course was developed to address a business need for an efficient training solution. By applying the Action Mapping approach, I prioritised what employees needed to do. I designed real-world scenarios and interactive practice activities to reinforce food safety practices. The module achieved a reduction in cross-contamination incidents, while earning positive feedback from both users and supervisors.
