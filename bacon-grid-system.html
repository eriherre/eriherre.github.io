Bacon Grid System

This e-learning module was developed to help people learn an improved and more efficient way of doing things. The instruction emphasises the contrast between an old and a new (improved) procedure. This module was built based on three related work departments to support trainees’ learning. The personnel from the three departments were not fully aware of the workflow and responsibilities in common. Thus, they did not know how their specific actions impacted the outcome. This instruction provided an overview of the workflow to support that knowledge-based gap, commonly familiar to many processing industries. This e-learning module also informs the steps that support the new procedure and its corrective actions. Finally, every section ends with a quiz to engage the audience and test knowledge retention.


Many were the motivations that drove the management force to re-think an already established working process. Nevertheless, those reasons are commonly associated with a more cost-effective solution. Once implemented, this instruction became an essential training resource that was regularly supported by one-on-one supervision and on-job-trainers. The new system proved cost-effective by speeding up the working process and reducing workplace injuries. This training resource was developed using Storyline 360 and Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing.
