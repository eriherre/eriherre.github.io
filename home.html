Home

Home is an interactive simulation designed for people who love to chill out beside a fireplace. People often say nothing is more satisfying than watching a fire on a rainy day. Well, we got you covered. Adjust the lighting and weather conditions with only one click and enjoy the sound of nature. Simple things like listening to music or feeding our pets can make us happy. If that get’s your attention, we invite you to combine all these unique moments and live together one incredible experience.


Regarding the user interface (UI), the main menu button (top left corner) allows the users to change the duration of the fire, mute all sounds, highlight the interactable objects and exit the application. The camera button (bottom right corner) allows switching the view. The user can select four different perspectives. The cat shape button located on the top allows changing the cat fur. The stock or inventory buttons (top right corner) inform the user how many supplies are left. There are several interactive objects in this application. All of them will be highlighted at the start. Some items (e.g., windows) will display a different UI (top centre) when pressed, allowing the user to experiment with other settings.

Users must press the items to initiate any action (e.g., animate interactable or get feedback). We provide users with an initial stock. Logs can be placed into the fireplace and lighted up using matchsticks. The pet can also be fed whenever feels convenient. Once the users run out of supplies (e.i., wood, pet food, matchsticks), we offer them to watch a rewarded video to get re-stock. This mobile application was developed using the Unity3D engine and can be downloaded from Google Play.
