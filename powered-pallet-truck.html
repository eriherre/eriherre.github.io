Powered Pallet Truck

This e-learning module was developed to help people learn how to safely inspect, operate and clean a powered pallet truck (PPT). The PPTs are generally used in many industries to move heavy loads from one point to another. Moreover, as with many other electric vehicles, only competent operators are authorised to drive them.


This interactive instruction covers critical safety components around the vehicle itself and the environment where it can be operated. The main sections are classified into three categories:

  1. Pre-Op Inspection, where trainees learn how to inspect all the vehicle parts and test the controls.
  2. Operation, designed to help people learn how to keep themselves and others safe while driving.
  3. Cleaning, where trainees learn how to maintain the PPT clean and ready for each use.

Finally, the course ends with a quiz to engage the audience and test knowledge retention. This e-learning module was developed using Storyline 360 and Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing.
